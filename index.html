01 — About

Curious by default. Calm under complexity.

I’m drawn to new challenges and unfamiliar stacks. Every domain — ERP, healthcare, integrations, internal platforms — has its own language and constraints, and I enjoy learning them quickly enough to ship something useful, then iterating with the team.

My approach is deliberately language-agnostic and tool-pragmatic. I choose the right runtime, framework, and pattern for the problem — not the other way around. Lately, that means a lot of agentic workflows, AI-augmented automation, and event-driven integration alongside the classics.

I care about reliability, clarity, and scale. The systems I’m most proud of are the ones you don’t notice — they just keep working as the business grows.
